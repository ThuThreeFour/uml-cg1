=== How to use application ===
1. Look for Step 1: Add texture
2. Follow the direction and choose exactly 2 textures. Your choices are Earth, cloud, Earth bump, and Moon bump.
3. Click the orange "Draw" button.
4. The canvas should render along with a sphere object that is spinning around.
5. Use your mouse or track pad to zoom in object.
6. Use your mouse or track pad to drag around the object.
7. The is a control to adjust the rotation speed at the top right corner of the screen.
