Programming Assignment 1: 2D Drawing

Line Circle Ellipse Rectangle Polygons & Polylines

Draw a line segment by clicking and dragging. To stop drawing, click again to lock the shape.
To clear the canvas click clear or click on the canvas.


To draw a circle, first, you must draw a circle's diameter by clicking and
dragging out a line, then click again to lock the shape.


Click on the canvas to draw an ellipse.


Draw a rectangle. Drag the blue dots to resize (not implemented yet)


Draw polygons and polylines by clicking, dragging the line, click to lock in
position, repeat the step. Double click to stop drawing.
